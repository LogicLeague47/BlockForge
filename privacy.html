Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Overview

BlockForge ("the Game", "we", "us") is a browser-based voxel building game. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data Stored On Your Device

The Game stores the following information locally in your browser using localStorage and IndexedDB:

• Your chosen username and player settings
• Your game world (terrain, structures, inventory, and progress)
• Custom skin data you create
• Locally cached account credentials used to log in
• Voice chat preferences (when voice chat is enabled)

This data never leaves your device unless you voluntarily join an online multiplayer server (see below).

Multiplayer Servers

When you join a BlockForge multiplayer server, the following is transmitted to that server:

• Your username and public skin selection
• Your position, movement, and actions within the game world
• Chat messages you send
• Your account credentials (username and password) to verify your login
• Voice chat audio (when voice chat is enabled; peer-to-peer via WebRTC)

Server operators may log connection information. We are not responsible for data handled by third-party servers.

Account Data

If you create an account, your password is hashed using the scrypt algorithm with a unique salt before being stored. Plaintext passwords are never stored or transmitted after the initial login request.

Third-Party Services

When played on CrazyGames, the CrazyGames SDK may collect analytics and advertising data under its own privacy policy. Please review CrazyGames' policy for details.

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond the locally stored game data described above.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer at the support address listed on the game's main menu.
